Why Contractors Choose Doortronix

Engineered to eliminate the problems that drive up your callback costs — and your customers' frustration.

🔩

No Track. No Wheels. No Callbacks.

The Revolution system uses a 1" chrome steel rod with a linear motion bearing instead of a track-and-roller system. "Door off track" causes 85% of service calls — we've eliminated it entirely.

🏗️

I-Beam Header — Zero Deflection

Our structural I-beam header doesn't sag under load like extruded aluminum headers. No deflection means panels stay aligned for the life of the door — no shimming, no seasonal adjustments.

💻

Self-Diagnosing Controller

The microprocessor controller self-diagnoses and displays fault codes on an LED readout. No handheld programmer required — technicians don't need special equipment for service calls.

🔓

Magnetic Breakaway Sidelights

Sidelights release magnetically — no ball catches, no mechanical latches. The full sidelight releases instantly in an emergency and resets easily without tools.

🇺🇸

Electronics Made in USA

Our control boards and electronics are manufactured domestically. Factory-direct pricing means no distributor markup — you get better margin or pass savings to your customer.

🛡️

Industry-Leading Warranty

5-year limited warranty on the complete system, plus a lifetime warranty on the rod and bearing — the core components that make our system fundamentally different.
