ASHRAE 170 AAADM Certified Infection Control

Hermetic Automatic Sliding Door

Full perimeter sealing for operating rooms, ICUs, cleanrooms, and pharmaceutical facilities. Continuous compression gaskets. <0.05 cfm/sq ft air leakage.

Designed for the Strictest Infection Control Environments

Operating rooms, isolation rooms, and cleanrooms require more than a standard automatic door — they require a controlled pressure boundary. Our hermetic door system maintains that boundary automatically every cycle.

The hermetic model is built on the same proven DT-3000 drive platform — rod and bearing, I-beam header, self-diagnosing controller — with the addition of full perimeter compression sealing on all four sides of the panel.

  • Continuous compression gaskets — all four sides, full height
  • Air leakage <0.05 cfm per square foot at 0.1" w.g.
  • Meets ASHRAE 170 pressure requirements
  • AAADM-certified installation program
  • Electronic interlock available (one door at a time)
  • All stainless hardware — cleanroom & infection control compatible
  • Lead-lined panels available for radiology environments
  • Same rod-and-bearing reliability — no track to contaminate

Healthcare Applications

🏥

Operating Rooms

Positive or negative pressure environments. Electronic interlock prevents simultaneous opening between sterile and non-sterile corridors. Full seal on closure.

🧪

Isolation / ICU Rooms

Negative pressure isolation rooms per CDC guidelines. Door seals completely to maintain pressure differential and contain airborne pathogens.

💊

Pharmacy & Cleanroom

USP 797/800 compliant ante-room and buffer room applications. Stainless hardware withstands chemical cleaners and disinfectants.

☢️

Radiology

Lead-lined panel option available. Provides radiation shielding to specified Pb equivalency while maintaining automatic operation.
