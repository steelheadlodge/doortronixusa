All-Glass Automatic Sliding Door

Maximum transparency. Minimal frame. The same proven rod-and-bearing drive system in a full-glass frameless aesthetic for high-image entrances.

Frameless Glass — Full Reliability

The All-Glass system uses a minimal header profile to conceal the drive mechanism while exposing maximum glass. From the outside, it looks like a wall of glass. From the inside, it still rides on the same 1-inch chrome steel rod and linear motion bearing that's eliminated "door off track" for our commercial customers.

Ideal for upscale retail, hotel main entrances, medical office lobbies, and any application where the door needs to make a first impression.

  • Frameless tempered glass panels — no exposed frame at edge
  • Minimal header conceals the drive mechanism
  • Rod-and-bearing drive — same 10M cycle reliability
  • Bi-parting and single-slide configurations
  • Stainless or anodized hardware options
  • Low-E and tinted glass options available
  • ADA-compliant timing and activation
  • Battery backup option
