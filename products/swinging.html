Automatic Swinging Door

ADA-compliant automatic swing door operator for new construction and retrofit. Single and double-leaf. Interior and exterior rated. Push-and-go or touchless activation.

Where Sliding Isn't an Option

Not every opening has room for a sliding pocket. Vestibule corners, narrow corridors, and retrofit applications often call for an automatic swing door. Our swing door system delivers full ADA compliance without sacrificing the factory-direct pricing and Texas-built quality you get across our entire product line.

  • ADA / ANSI A117.1 compliant activation force and timing
  • Low-energy mode (push-and-go) and touchless mode
  • Single-leaf and double-leaf configurations
  • Interior and exterior ratings available
  • Works with most standard commercial door frames
  • Adjustable opening speed and hold-open time
  • Obstruction sensing — auto-reverse on contact
  • Battery backup option
