Doortronix has produced automatic door systems in East Texas for over three decades. When you specify Doortronix, you're buying from an American manufacturer — not an American-branded product made overseas.

Our American Manufacturing Claims

We take our origin claims seriously. Below are the specific claims we make about Doortronix products — and what they mean. We believe in accurate, honest communication about where our products are made.

✅ "Manufactured in Malakoff, Texas"
Our production facility is located in 5783 County Road 1219, Malakoff, TX 75148. Door systems are assembled, wired, and quality-tested here before shipment.

✅ "Built in Texas • Delivered Nationwide"
Custom-configured in our Texas facility. Shipped to job sites across the continental United States.

✅ "American Manufacturer"
Doortronix Inc. is an American company, operated by American employees, with domestic manufacturing operations.

✅ "Factory-Direct from our Texas Production Facility"
We sell directly to contractors — no domestic or foreign distribution intermediary.

Our electronic components are manufactured domestically. As with most manufacturers in the automatic door industry, some hardware components are sourced globally. We will always give you an accurate answer if you have specific sourcing questions for your project.

Government Projects — TAA / BAA Compliance

Working on a federal project with Buy American Act or Trade Agreements Act requirements? Contact us before you bid.

🏛️

Buy American Act (BAA)

Federal construction projects subject to BAA require domestic manufactured products. Our Texas-manufactured door systems can qualify. Contact us with your project details and we'll provide the documentation your purchasing office needs.

🤝

Trade Agreements Act (TAA)

GSA schedules and certain federal contracts require TAA-compliant products. We can provide product origin documentation and work with your compliance team to meet TAA requirements.

🎖️

VA & Military Facilities

We have a strong track record of supplying automatic door systems to VA medical centers, military bases, and federal facilities. Our products are in service at multiple VA locations nationwide.

Need compliance documentation? Contact us with your project specs, contract number, and compliance requirement. We'll provide the manufacturer's origin certification and any supporting documentation needed for your procurement process.
